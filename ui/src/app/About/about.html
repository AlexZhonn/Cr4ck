about cr4ck

Learn OOP by building real systems

Cr4ck is an AI-powered practice platform for developers who want to go beyond LeetCode. Write real object-oriented code, then get instant architectural feedback from Claude.

Real design challenges

Not toy problems — design a library system, a rate limiter, a notification service. The kind of things you'd actually build at work.

AI architectural feedback

Submit your code and get a score, strengths, and specific improvements — covering encapsulation, SOLID principles, and system design trade-offs.

Progress tracking

Earn XP for every submission. Track your streak. Watch your challenges_completed count grow as you level up your design skills.

Multi-language support

Write in Java, TypeScript, Python, or C++ — whichever language fits the challenge. Monaco Editor with full syntax highlighting.
